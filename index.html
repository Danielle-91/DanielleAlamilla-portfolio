Projects

  • Record + Play

    Remember waiting in front of the radio for your favourite songs to come on so you could scramble to record them onto a blank cassette tape? Remember yelling very bad words when the DJ started talking before the song was done? Cuss no more! Record + Play is a mixtape-maker created in React using the iTunes API. Type in the name of your favourite artists and it will return a list of ten of their songs for you to use as your very own mixtape. I'm excited to play around with this one some more and add to its functionality!

  • Around the World Flag Quiz

    Did you know at least one colour from the Olympic rings can be found on the flag of every country in the world? There are five colours but many, many, many...MANY flags. Kelvin Lee and I created a quiz to test your knowledge on these flags using vanilla JavaScript and the Rest Countries API. Give it a try!
